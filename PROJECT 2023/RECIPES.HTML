Avocado Toast

A delicious and nutritious breakfast option.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, poached egg, radishes

Instructions:

  1. Toast the slices of bread until golden brown.
  2. Mash the ripe avocado and spread it evenly on the toast.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Add your favorite toppings.
  5. Enjoy!

Quinoa Salad

A refreshing and protein-packed salad.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • Handful of fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, and basil.
  2. Drizzle olive oil over the salad and toss until well combined.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve chilled and enjoy!
