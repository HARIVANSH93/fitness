Gym Exercises

Deadlifts

Deadlifts are a fundamental compound exercise that targets multiple muscle groups, including the back, legs, and core. They are great for building overall strength and improving posture.

Push-ups

Push-ups are a classic bodyweight exercise that works the chest, shoulders, triceps, and core. They are effective for building upper body strength and endurance.

Squats

Squats are a compound exercise that targets the muscles of the lower body, including the quads, hamstrings, and glutes. They are excellent for building leg strength and enhancing overall athleticism.
